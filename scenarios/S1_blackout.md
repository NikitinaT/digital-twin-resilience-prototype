# S1 — Regional Blackout (Infrastructure Stress)

**Trigger:** upstream regional grid instability / blackout.

**Expected KPI impact (illustrative):**
- Energy: 5–15%
- Connectivity: 10–30%
- Academic continuity: 0–15%
- Critical services: ~40% (minimal fallback)

**DT actions (high-level):**
- detect drop in supply/telemetry,
- switch to emergency mode,
- initiate tier-based allocation,
- request/activate on-site sources (battery, generator, microgrid).

**Output:** prepare transition to S3 once DT control loop is active.
