# S2 — Physical Attack on One Building (Structural Disruption)

**Trigger:** localized damage to a facility (e.g., lab/teaching building).

**Expected KPI impact (illustrative):**
- Infrastructure integrity: 70–85% (campus-wide)
- Academic continuity: 60–85% (depends on affected building)
- Connectivity: 70–90% (if comms nodes affected, lower)
- Energy: 40–70% (if local distribution impacted)

**DT actions (high-level):**
- zone labeling (damaged / safe),
- restrict access and reroute flows,
- reschedule classes and reassign labs,
- dispatch inspection (human/drone), update damage estimates.
